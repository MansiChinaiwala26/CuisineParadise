food made with love

Cuisine Paradise is a contemporary restaurant located atop a heritage building in downtown Toronto. Our dining room offers blind, multi-course tasting menus that highlight the finest in seasonal ingredients, while our adjacent barroom features classically-inspired cocktails and a concise à la carte menu.

our speciality

burger

A huge single or triple burger with all the fixings, cheese, lettuce, tomato, onions and special sauce or mayonnaise!

pizza

Bread dough topped with some combination of olive oil, oregano, tomato, olives, mozzarella or other cheese, and many other ingredients, baked quickly using wood fire.

Strawberry ice-cream
